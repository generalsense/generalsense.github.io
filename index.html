Sentiment Analysis

General Sense uses a branch of artificial intelligence called "sentiment analysis." This enables computers to identify public attitudes towards a given issue. These insights are otherwise imperceptible to humans and enable better decision making. Additionally, users can upload or select other data sets to check for correlation.

EXAMPLE:
78% of the time that sentiment towards issue X falls below level Y there are public protest and/or riots in city Z.

Security & Privacy First
